Our Story

Founded in the heart of New York City, Metro Gourmet Catering & Events has been elevating celebrations and corporate gatherings for over a decade. What began as a passion for bringing people together through exceptional food has grown into one of NYC's most trusted catering services.

Our journey started with a simple belief: every event deserves extraordinary cuisine. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we've dedicated ourselves to creating memorable dining experiences that reflect the diverse culinary landscape of New York City.

Today, Metro Gourmet stands as a testament to quality, creativity, and impeccable service. Our team of culinary artisans combines traditional techniques with innovative approaches, sourcing the finest ingredients to craft menus that delight and inspire.
